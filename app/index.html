About Daniel Marino

I'm an award-winning product designer who is passionate about creating simple, high-quality user experiences.

During the day I push pixels and wrangle code for Cramer Dev as a product designer from my home in New Hampshire. Over the years my work has been featured in several industry publications and style guides including CSS Mania, Pattern Tap, Media Queries, and more. I've also won an American Inhouse Design Award from Graphic Design USA.

When I'm not workin' for the man, I enjoy spending time with my wife and three kids. I also like rockin' out on guitar and drums, reading a good book, and savoring tender moments with my cat.

If you'd like a more formal background, please check out my résumé.
